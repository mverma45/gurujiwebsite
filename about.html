About GuruJi

GuruJi, whose real name is Nirmal Singh, was born on July 7, 1954, in Dugri village, Malerkotla, Sangrur District, Punjab, India. He is a revered spiritual leader known for his teachings on meditation, mindfulness, and inner peace.

With a lifelong commitment to guiding individuals on their spiritual journeys, GuruJi has inspired people across the world. His satsangs offer wisdom, calmness, and practical guidance for living a balanced and meaningful life.

Through his compassionate approach and deep spiritual insight, GuruJi continues to touch hearts and transform lives, helping seekers move toward greater awareness and inner peace.
