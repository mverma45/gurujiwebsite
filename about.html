About Guru Ji

Spiritual wisdom, devotion, meditation, and inner peace.

Guru Ji, whose real name is Nirmal Singh, was born on July 7, 1954, in Dugri village, Malerkotla, Sangrur District, Punjab, India.

He is a revered spiritual leader known for his teachings on meditation, mindfulness, devotion, and inner peace. Through his satsangs, Guru Ji inspired many people to live with faith, gratitude, humility, and love.

His sangat continues to gather across the world to remember his blessings, listen to shabads, and experience the peace of Guru Ji’s presence.
